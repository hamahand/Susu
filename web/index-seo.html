Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Susu (ROSCA)?

+

A ROSCA (Rotating Savings and Credit Association), known as "Susu" in Ghana, is a traditional savings system where a group of people contribute a fixed amount regularly. Each period, one member receives the total pot, rotating until everyone has received their payout.

How much does SusuSave cost?

+

SusuSave is completely free to use. We don't charge any platform fees. You only pay standard Mobile Money transaction fees charged by your network provider.

Which Mobile Money providers are supported?

+

Currently, we support MTN Mobile Money. Support for Vodafone Cash and AirtelTigo Money is coming soon.

What happens if I miss a payment?

+

If your automatic payment fails (e.g., insufficient balance), we'll automatically retry up to 3 times and send you SMS reminders. Make sure you have sufficient balance before your contribution date.

Can I leave a group early?

+

You should complete the full rotation cycle before leaving. If you must leave early, discuss with your group admin. Outstanding contributions will need to be settled.

How secure is my money?

+

Your money never sits with SusuSave. All transactions go directly through Mobile Money. We use bank-grade encryption for all sensitive data and maintain complete audit trails. Your phone number and financial data are encrypted.
